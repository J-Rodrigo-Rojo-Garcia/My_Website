About

Hello,

Hi, I am Rodrigo and I am a PhD candidate at Lappeenranta University of Technology of Finland. At the same time I have a position as Jr. Researcher in the same university.

My research area is Bayesian Inverse Problem Theory and my interests are focused in the study and application of algorithms for the solution of industrial problems.

I have experience working with simulations based on: Bayesian Statistics, Kalman Filter, SDEs, Regression Analysis, Optimization algorithms, Monte Carlo simulations and sampling techniques with Markov Chain Monte Carlo methods.

Here you will find information about my background, research, scientific publications and conferences in which I have participated. Also you can take a look of some personal projects and coding that I have developed.
